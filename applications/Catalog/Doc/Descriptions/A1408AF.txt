Chet Reneson is a sporting artist most noted for his watercolor scenes of duck hunting in the biting cold and wind
<i>Bad Timing</i> shows a pair of duck hunters weathering the elements with their faithful black lab
Reneson was named the 1982 DU Artist of the Year with his image <i>Hunters of the Marsh</i>
Limited edition giclee signed by the artist and printed on watercolor paper with a title plate inlaid in the mat
