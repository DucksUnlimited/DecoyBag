2013/2014 Federal Duck Stamp created by artist Robert Steiner
Print and stamp image are of a common goldeneye swimming on the water
This is the second Federal Duck Stamp win for Steiner, who has earned a reputation as one of the most accomplished duck stamp artists with over 85 state duck stamp wins
Framed, double matted, and inlaid with a DU crest medallion and carved feather, exclusive to DU events
