Four piece knife collection highlighting the four waterfowl flyway regions 
Knives feature the Mississippi Flyway with mallard, Pacific Flyway with pintail, Central Flyway with blue-winged teal, and Atlantic Flyway with canvasback
The name of the waterfowl species and flyway are engraved on the blade of each knife and an image of the duck is embossed on the handle
Includes a DU medallion in the center of the piece
The black shadowbox frame has a custom felt burgundy lining
Includes two nail holes for wall display
